About us

Through the rich history of Golden Tech Group since 2012

Golden Tech Group began by helping UAE residents and workers manage visas, Emirates ID renewals, and government paperwork with speed and accuracy. Working closely with that community made one thing clear: the same families needed reliable travel arrangements back home, and the same job-seekers needed proper typing skills before they ever reached an interview.

We offer a wide range of Typing Services, Business setup, Business Management Services, Travel & Tourism packages, Interview Placement assistance, and comprehensive Typing Training courses that lead straight to career success.
