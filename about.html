About Us

Built on the route millions of Keralites already travel

Golden Tech Group started as a documentation office in the UAE and grew into a full corridor of services — because the paperwork, the training, and the journey are rarely separate problems.

Our Story

One office, then a corridor

Golden Tech Group began by helping UAE residents and workers manage visas, Emirates ID renewals, and government paperwork with speed and accuracy. Working closely with that community made one thing clear: the same families needed reliable travel arrangements back home, and the same job-seekers needed proper typing skills before they ever reached an interview.

That's why we opened a second branch in Kerala — combining travel & tourism services with a dedicated typing training institute, so the path from "I want to work in the UAE" to "I have the job" runs through a single, trusted office.

Our Mission

Make cross-border paperwork, travel, and career preparation simple, honest, and fast for every family we serve.

Our Vision

To be the first name Kerala-UAE families think of for documentation, travel, and job-ready training.
